Our Story

Step into RAGAZZI and embark on an authentic culinary journey through the rich flavours of Italy. Our esteemed owner, Leonardo Placidi, draws from his cherished family heritage, where he learned the secrets of traditional Italian cuisine.

Join us to savour the warmth and hospitality of Italy, where each bite brings us together to create indelible memories.

Our Food

Inspired by the simplicity and soul of Italian borgos — those charming mountain villages — we believe in crafting dishes that celebrate the finest, freshest ingredients.

From timeless pasta creations to wood-fired pizzas and exquisite seafood, our menu honours Italy's culinary legacy while embracing innovation for a vibrant dining experience.

Our Events

Indulge in a dining experience that transcends the ordinary with our thoughtfully curated events, promising unforgettable moments around the table. From enchanting themed culinary nights to the soulful melodies of live music evenings, we've woven an ambiance that welcomes you like family.
